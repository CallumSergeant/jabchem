Welcome to JABchem

The legal stuff.

SQA Past Papers and SQA Marking Schemes can only be used to support study for SQA qualifications and cannot be used for any other purpose without their permission. Other materials published here are copyright of JABchem. Learners can use them to support their studies but please no sharing online. Tell them about this website instead. Educational establishments must ask permission to use these materials and an exchange of materials would be expected in return.
